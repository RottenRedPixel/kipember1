## Description

Ember helps families and friends preserve the meaning behind photos, videos, and shared memories.

Start with a single image or moment, then invite the people who were there to add their perspective by text or voice. Ember keeps every contribution connected to the same memory so the story grows over time instead of getting lost in scattered messages and photo captions.

Use Ember to:

- upload photos and videos into a shared memory archive
- invite family and friends into a guided Story Circle
- collect voice interviews and written reflections
- generate living memory pages with context, people, places, and meaning
- revisit memories through chat, summaries, and child-friendly story views

Ember is built for preserving family history, oral storytelling, and the emotional detail that usually disappears from a photo library.
